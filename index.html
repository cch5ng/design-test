My Story

I'm an avid coder, reader, and writer. Out of college I worked as a software Technical Writer after a brief stint in journalism. Although I enjoyed the opportunity to learn on the job, after 10 years of working in different companies, including startups, I needed new challenges.

I enrolled in an online JavaScript (vanilla) course and really enjoyed it. I decided to transition to Front End Developer work. I took courses, completing the Udacity and FreeCodeCamp Front End Web Developer certificates. Then I became a Code Reviewer for Udacity and am currently working for Udacity Blitz as a Front End Web Developer building a job candidate search tool.

My favorite aspect of Front End Development is the ability to combine both psychological thinking (user needs, UI design) along with technical problem solving skills. I still really enjoy JavaScript and am currently working with React and Redux.
